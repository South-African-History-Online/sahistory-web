## Claims flagged for SME verification

- **[SME] 1948 election result mechanics** (s3): the assertion that the National
  Party won a parliamentary majority while receiving fewer total votes than the
  United Party is historically accepted, but the exact seat and vote figures and
  the role of the Afrikaner Party alliance should be confirmed against a primary
  reference before any numbers are added.
- **[SME] Verwoerd's stated aim for Bantu Education** (s8): the deck paraphrases
  rather than quotes Verwoerd. If a direct quotation is added, it must be
  verified verbatim and cited.
- **[SME] Defiance Campaign scale** (s10): the deck avoids a specific arrest
  figure (commonly cited around 8,000-8,500 volunteers). Confirm before adding a
  number.
- **[SME] Sophiatown removals scale** (s11): total number of people removed
  (often cited around 60,000 over the full period) is intentionally omitted;
  confirm before adding.
- **[SME] Women's March crowd size** (s13): "about 20,000" is the widely cited
  figure and matches SAHO; confirm the wording ("about"/"approximately") is
  acceptable for the SME.
- **[SME] Act numbers** (s5-s8): Act numbers (No. 30, 41, 44 of 1950; No. 47 of
  1953) are standard but should be spot-checked against the linked SAHO pages.
- **[SME] Reading level and terminology**: confirm the Grade 9 reading level and
  that sensitive terminology (e.g. the historical term "Native") is handled with
  the framing the SME prefers for learners.
